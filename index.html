Welcome to Lasclottes

Nestled on the banks of the River Lot in south-west France, Lasclottes is a beautifully restored property set within five acres of private gardens. The Granary and Annex offer five bedrooms sleeping up to 12 across two adjoining self-contained buildings, with a private pool and 150 metres of private river frontage — the perfect retreat for families, groups of friends, and fishing enthusiasts.

The Property

The Granary & Annex — five bedrooms sleeping up to 12 guests

The Granary & Annex

Two beautifully converted adjoining buildings adjacent to the main farmhouse, offering self-contained accommodation across five bedrooms and four bathrooms. Each has its own private entrance and living area — perfect for families or groups of friends who want both togetherness and independence.

The Annex features a charming lounge with red leather sofas, an en-suite bedroom, and garden views. The Granary offers exposed beams, an open-plan living space with kitchen, and stairs to the upper bedrooms. Three of the five bedrooms have en-suite facilities, and two of the bedrooms share the family bathroom.

  • 5 bedrooms (sleeps 12)
  • 4 bathrooms (3 en-suite)
  • Kitchen & living areas
  • Private entrances & terraces

The main farmhouse is currently being renovated and will be available to rent from 2028. Once complete, it will add two further bedrooms, a bathroom, another kitchen, a lounge area, and a living area — bringing the total property to seven bedrooms and five bathrooms.

The House and Garden

The swimming pool is set within the garden with views over the grounds and down to the river. Loungers and parasols are available — the perfect place to spend lazy afternoons.

  • Swimming pool
  • Loungers & parasols

River & Gardens

The five-acre grounds stretch down to the River Lot, with 150 metres of private river frontage. Two pontoons provide access for fishing and launching canoes or kayaks.

The gardens include manicured lawns, fruit orchards, a pétanque pitch (similar to bowls), and shaded seating areas — with wildlife including herons, kingfishers, and black kites regularly spotted.

  • 150m river frontage
  • Two pontoons
  • Pétanque pitch
  • Orchards & gardens

Things To Do

From lazy river days to exploring medieval villages

Fishing

Fish directly from our private pontoons on the River Lot — carp to 44lb, catfish to 83lb, and a designated night fishing zone right past the property.

Cycling

Explore the Lot valley on quiet country roads and dedicated cycle paths. The Véloroute du Lot runs right past the property. Bike hire available locally.

Canoeing & Kayaking

Launch from our pontoon or hire canoes and leisure boats at nearby Penne d'Agenais for scenic river trips through the valley.

Golf

Several courses within easy reach, including Golf de Villeneuve-sur-Lot. Enjoy a round amidst the rolling hills and vineyards of Lot-et-Garonne.

Markets & Dining

Weekly markets in Villeneuve-sur-Lot and surrounding villages. Fresh local produce, artisan goods, and excellent restaurants.

Wine & Châteaux

Visit local vineyards for tastings, or take a day trip to the renowned wine regions of Bordeaux, Cahors, or Bergerac.

Medieval Villages

Explore bastide towns and hilltop perched villages. Pujols, Penne d'Agenais, and Tournon d'Agenais are all within easy reach.

Hot Air Balloons

Hot air balloons occasionally launch from nearby fields — if you're lucky, you may spot them drifting over the garden.

Water Sports

Waterskiing, wakeboarding, and leisure boating at nearby bases on the Lot.
