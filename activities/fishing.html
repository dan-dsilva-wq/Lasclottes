Fishing at Lasclottes

Private river fishing on the Lot — directly from your doorstep

Lasclottes sits on the banks of the River Lot, one of southwest France's finest coarse fishing rivers. With private pontoons, over 200 metres of riverbank, and a designated night fishing zone running directly past the property, it's a destination that anglers return to year after year.

What You Can Catch

The River Lot holds exceptional stocks of carp, with fish regularly caught to 44 lb and beyond from the Lasclottes pontoons. Catfish (silure) are abundant, with specimens to 83 lb landed here. The river also holds bream, tench, pike, perch, and zander.

Whether you're a specimen hunter after big carp or a family looking for a fun afternoon by the river, there's something for everyone. The stretch at Lasclottes is particularly productive thanks to its depth, cover, and year-round flow.

Night Fishing

The River Lot has a designated 6 km night carp fishing zone running from Saint-Sylvestre-sur-Lot directly past the property. Night fishing is heavily restricted across France, making this a rare privilege for visiting anglers.

Set up on the pontoon as the sun goes down, and fish through the warm summer nights with nothing but the sound of the river and the occasional splash of a feeding carp. It's an experience unlike any other.

Your Fishing Setup

Lasclottes has two private fishing pontoons on the river, giving you direct access without needing to travel anywhere. You can fish from the moment you arrive until the moment you leave.

Bring your own gear, or hire directly from us. We can provide rods, reels, nets, and basic tackle so you can travel light and still enjoy a full fishing holiday.

There's also a river beach area perfect for younger anglers, where smaller fish can be caught on simple float rigs — a great way to introduce children to fishing.

Practical Information

Fishing Licence

A carte de pêche is required to fish in France. Available online at cartedepeche.fr or from local tackle shops. Day cards from approximately €15, weekly from €32.

Local Tackle Shops

Villeneuve Pêche and Pêche Attitude in Villeneuve-sur-Lot (15 minutes) stock bait, tackle, and can advise on local conditions.

Guided Fishing

Local guides including MR Fishing France and Lot Experience offer guided sessions on the Lot if you'd like expert help finding the best swims.

Equipment Hire

We offer rod and tackle hire directly at the property. Let us know what you need when you book and we'll have everything ready for your arrival.
