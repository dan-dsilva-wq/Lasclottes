Golf near Lasclottes

Several courses within easy reach of the property

The Lot-et-Garonne and surrounding départements have a good selection of golf courses, from full 18-hole championship layouts to charming 9-hole courses set in château grounds. The nearest is just 15 minutes from Lasclottes, and there are five courses within 40 minutes — enough for a different round almost every day of the week.

Courses Near Lasclottes

Golf de Villeneuve-sur-Lot

15–20 minutes from Lasclottes

The closest course and a real gem. An 18-hole, Par 72 layout set in beautiful rolling countryside with mature trees and water features. Well-maintained and welcoming to visitors.

Holes: 18 • Par: 72 Green fee: Approximately €62–€70 Club hire: Available

Golf de Barthe, Tombeboeuf

25–30 minutes from Lasclottes

A charming 9-hole course in rolling countryside surrounded by farms and gentle hills. Excellent greens, water hazards on several holes, and a friendly, relaxed atmosphere. Very affordable and welcoming to visitors of all levels. Can be played twice for an 18-hole round.

Holes: 9 • Par: 36 Green fee: €20–€35 Club hire: Available

Albret Golf Experience, Barbaste

35–40 minutes from Lasclottes

A full 18-hole, Par 72 course set amid century-old oaks with panoramic views over the Buzet vineyards and bastide villages. Sandy soil means it's playable year-round. Recently revitalised with a bistro restaurant, hotel, and padel courts on site.

Holes: 18 • Par: 72 Green fee: €34–€60 Club hire: Available

Golf d'Agen Bon-Encontre

30–35 minutes from Lasclottes

A hilly 9-hole course on high ground overlooking the Garonne valley near Agen. Suitable for all levels, with a driving range, putting green, restaurant, and pro shop. A good option for a half-day round.

Holes: 9 Green fee: €34 (9 holes) Club hire: Available

Golf du Château d'Allot, Agen-Boé

40 minutes from Lasclottes

The top-rated course in the Agen area, expanded to 18 holes in 2024. A modern Par 70 layout alongside the Garonne with century-old cedars and mature parkland. Full practice facilities and clubhouse.

Holes: 18 • Par: 70 Green fee: €39–€60 Club hire: Available

Practical Information

Booking Tee Times

Most courses accept walk-ins outside peak times, but booking ahead is recommended in July and August. Course websites and phone booking are both available.

Club Hire

All the main courses offer club hire. If you're travelling light, you can still enjoy a round without bringing your own equipment.

Best Time to Play

Early morning tee times are best in summer. May, June, and September offer the most comfortable playing conditions. Most courses are open year-round.

Handicap Certificates

Some courses may ask for a handicap certificate, though many welcome all levels. Check with the individual course when booking if you're unsure.
