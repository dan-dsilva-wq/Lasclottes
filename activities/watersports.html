Water Sports

Waterskiing, wakeboarding, and leisure boating on the Lot

The River Lot and the lakes of the Lot-et-Garonne offer a range of water sports for thrill-seekers and families alike. From waterskiing and wakeboarding to leisure boating and open-water swimming, there's plenty to get you on (or in) the water during your stay at Lasclottes.

What's Available

Waterskiing

The Club Nautique Penne Saint-Sylvestre offers waterskiing sessions on the Lot, just minutes from Lasclottes. Suitable for all levels, from complete beginners to experienced skiers. Equipment and instruction are provided.

Wakeboarding

Wakeboarding is also available at the Club Nautique. The calm, wide sections of the Lot are ideal for learning, and the club's instructors can get beginners up and riding within a session or two.

Leisure Boating

Electric boats and leisure craft can be hired for gentle cruises on the Lot. A peaceful way to spend an afternoon, drifting past the fields and villages that line the river. No licence required for small electric boats.

Open-Water Swimming

The floating swimming pool (piscine flottante) at Saint-Sylvestre-sur-Lot is a unique facility — a proper swimming pool floating on the river itself, with lifeguards and changing facilities. Open in summer and popular with families.

Where to Go

Club Nautique Penne Saint-Sylvestre

5–10 minutes from Lasclottes

The closest water sports centre to Lasclottes, offering waterskiing, wakeboarding, and boat hire on the River Lot. The club is welcoming to visitors and offers sessions for beginners through to advanced. This is the go-to spot for anyone wanting to try something more active on the water.

Piscine Flottante, Saint-Sylvestre-sur-Lot

5 minutes from Lasclottes

A floating outdoor swimming pool on the River Lot, open during the summer months. It offers the experience of swimming in the river environment with the safety and facilities of a proper pool. Supervised by lifeguards, with changing rooms and a small café. A unique and refreshing experience.

Lac de Fumel

20 minutes from Lasclottes

A supervised lake beach with swimming, pedalos, and picnic areas. The lake has a designated swimming zone with lifeguards in summer, plus grassy areas for sunbathing and a playground for children. A good option for a family day out away from the river.

Back at Lasclottes

After a morning of water sports, head back to Lasclottes and cool off in the private swimming pool, set in the garden with views over the grounds and down to the river.

The pool is the perfect place to spend lazy afternoons between activities — loungers and parasols are available, and you can keep an eye on the river below in case you spot a hot air balloon or a heron.

Practical Information

Booking

Water sports sessions at the Club Nautique should be booked in advance during peak season (July–August). Outside peak times, walk-ins are often possible. We can help arrange sessions when you book your stay.

What to Bring

Swimwear, suncream, and a towel. Equipment (skis, boards, wetsuits, life jackets) is provided by the operators. Bring water shoes for the river beach areas.

For Children

The floating pool and lake beaches are ideal for younger children. Waterskiing and wakeboarding are typically available for children aged 8 and over, depending on the operator.

Season

Water sports run from May to September. The floating pool and lake beaches are typically open from late June to early September. The river itself is accessible year-round.
