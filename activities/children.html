Children's Activities

Family days out around Lasclottes: parks, animals, nature, sport, and adventure

If you are planning a family holiday, the Lot-et-Garonne has excellent options for children of all ages. From animal parks and inflatable water attractions to karting, treetop courses, and nature walks, there is enough nearby to build a full week of family outings.

The list below focuses on attractions that guests ask about most often, all reachable from Lasclottes by car.

Walygator Sud-Ouest & Aqualand Agen

Around 45 minutes

A full day option with rides and water attractions. Good choice in hot weather when you want bigger family entertainment close to Agen.

Official Website

Parc en Ciel

Around 30 minutes

Tree-top adventure courses, family trails, and outdoor activities in a woodland setting. A strong option for mixed-age groups.

Official Website

Site Nature des Étangs de Ferrié

Nature area with water play in summer and easy walks. Great for younger children when you want a lighter half-day outing.

Planning Tips for Families

Mix Big and Small Days

Balance one major day (theme park) with one lighter local day (river, Ferrié site, village market).

Book Peak-Season Slots Early

For July and August, reserve key activities a few days in advance, especially park entries and karting sessions.

Use Weather-Proof Backups

Indoor play, cave visits, and shaded woodland parks are useful alternatives for very hot or unsettled days.
