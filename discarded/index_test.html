Welcome

Your own corner of rural France

Nestled on the banks of the River Lot, Lasclottes is a beautifully restored farmhouse and granary set within five acres of private gardens. With seven bedrooms, a private heated pool, and 150 metres of private river frontage, it is the perfect retreat for families, groups of friends, and fishing enthusiasts.

The house sits between Villeneuve-sur-Lot and Penne d'Agenais in the Lot-et-Garonne, one of the sunniest and least-touristed corners of south-west France. Hot air balloons drift overhead at dusk, kingfishers flash along the river, and the nearest bakery is a two-minute drive.

Private River Access

150m of frontage on the Lot with two pontoons for fishing, swimming, and launching canoes.

The Property

Two buildings, one unforgettable stay

The Farmhouse

The main house has been lovingly restored to combine original character with modern comforts. Downstairs you will find a spacious open-plan kitchen and dining area, a comfortable lounge with fireplace, and a ground-floor double bedroom with en-suite. Upstairs there are three further bedrooms — including a super-king master with en-suite — a family bathroom, and a cosy reading nook.

  • 4 bedrooms (sleeps 8)
  • 3 bathrooms
  • Open-plan kitchen & dining
  • Lounge with fireplace

The Granary

A beautifully converted barn adjacent to the farmhouse with its own entrance, terrace, kitchen, and living area. Three bedrooms and two bathrooms make it perfect for a second family or group of friends — with the option of togetherness or independence.

  • 3 bedrooms (sleeps 4–6)
  • 2 bathrooms
  • Own kitchen & living area
  • Private terrace

Pool & Gardens

The swimming pool sits on a sun-drenched terrace with views over the gardens and down to the river. Below, five acres of grounds stretch to the water’s edge — manicured lawns, fruit orchards, a pétanque pitch, and shaded seating areas, with herons and kingfishers as regular visitors.

  • Heated swimming pool
  • Sun terrace with loungers
  • Pétanque pitch
  • Orchards & gardens

The River

150 metres of private frontage on the River Lot with two pontoons — one for fishing, one for leisure. The river is renowned for its carp (to 44lb) and catfish (to 83lb), and is equally perfect for a morning swim or a lazy afternoon on the water.

  • 150m private river frontage
  • Two pontoons
  • Carp, catfish, pike & bream
  • Canoe & kayak access

Things To Do

From lazy river days to medieval villages

Fishing

Fish from our private pontoons — carp to 44lb, catfish to 83lb, plus bream, pike, and more. May, June, and September offer ideal conditions.

Cycling

Quiet country roads and the Véloroute du Lot run right past the property. Bike hire available locally.

Canoeing & Kayaking

Launch from our pontoon or hire canoes at nearby Penne d'Agenais.

Markets & Dining

Weekly markets, fresh local produce, and excellent restaurants in surrounding villages.

Wine & Châteaux

Local vineyards plus day trips to Bordeaux, Cahors, or Bergerac.

Medieval Villages

Pujols, Penne d'Agenais, and Tournon d'Agenais — all within easy reach.

Hot Air Balloons

Watch them drift over the garden at dawn and dusk, or book a flight yourself.

Water Sports

Waterskiing and wakeboarding at nearby bases on the Lot.
